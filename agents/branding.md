# Branding & Design System

## Design System

The site uses a strict design system to prevent "design creep" and keep the site feeling grounded.

### Hex Codes

- **Forest Green (Primary)**: `#2C5530` (Used for backgrounds, primary buttons)
- **Warm Cedar (Accent)**: `#A45A52` (Used for accents, highlights)
- **Parchment Cream (Background)**: `#F5F1E6` (Used for main background)
- **Espresso Brown (Typography)**: `#3B2B25` (Used for text)

### Typography

- **Headings**: Classic Serif (e.g., Merriweather). To honor the Austen influence.
- **Body**: Modern Sans-Serif (e.g., Inter). For digital readability.

### UI Style

- **No shadows or gradients.**
- Use flat, organic textures.
- Generous white space.
- **Thematic Locking**: Use Tailwind's theme config (`bg-forest`, `text-cedar`) rather than arbitrary values.

## Content Philosophy (The "Subtle" Mandate)

### Imagery

- Use realistic, high-quality photography of environments (landscapes, cafes, writing desks).
- **Avoid**: "Worldly" or "Lust-focused" stock imagery common in YA Romance.

### Mission

- The site serves an audience seeking romance rooted in character, sincerity, and positive values.
